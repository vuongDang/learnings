## Evaluation of content through llm interpretability

What is a harmful content? 
Literally it's content that produces harm:
- direct harm to the user (emotional harm since it's text)
- can be used to produce harm
- can motivate the user to produce harm

An idea is that the first case could be evaluated through llms interpretability.
Exposing a model to harmful remarks and see what neurons are activated.
These neurons would certainly be related to the concept of being hurt in models.
For more reliability it would be interesting to do this experiment across
diverse models
