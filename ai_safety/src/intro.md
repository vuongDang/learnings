# AI Safety

Ai Safety: make sure nothing "bad" can happen with AI

## Different Fields of AI Safety
- Input sanitization: create a sane training dataset to teach a model to be harmless
    - Input filtering 
    - Prevent data poisoning
    - Examples of harmful behaviour
- Alignment techniques: create models that are harmless
    - RLFH/RLAIF
    - Constitutional AI
    - Deliberative alignment
- Evaluations 
    - Responsible Scaling Policy
    - Unified evalution framework 
- Interpretability: understand/steer a model behaviour from its internals
    - Chain of Thought
    - Linear Probes 
    - Sparse Autoencoders 
- AI control: prevent untrusted model from causing harm
    - scalable oversight: in a context where ais are smarter than humans
