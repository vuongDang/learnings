# Scalable oversight

## Context 

Can we have control mechanisms that still work when AIs grow even more intelligent?
How do you verify that an AI is safe when it's smarter than humans?
- as AIs becomes more powerful it's getting harder for humans to verify their tasks
- necessary to develop if we want AGI 

## Ideas 

- Splitting tasks into smaller ones that a human can verify
- Having a debates between AI on a task, this debate can help the human evaluating the task completion
- Having a helper AI that helps the human in the evaluation process
- Having a weaker but trusted AI be the judge for the evaluated AI
- Interpretability to understand the model reasoning and detect deception
